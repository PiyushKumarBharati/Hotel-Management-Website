Our Story

MNP Hotels began with a vision to redefine luxury hospitality. Founded in 2010, we’ve grown into a global brand with hotels in over 20 countries. Our journey has been fueled by passion, innovation, and a deep commitment to creating unforgettable experiences for every guest.

From a single boutique hotel to a network of premium properties, MNP Hotels has continuously evolved by embracing modern design, sustainable practices, and personalized guest services. Each hotel reflects the local culture while maintaining our signature standard of excellence.

We pride ourselves on nurturing long-term relationships with our guests, partners, and communities. Our legacy is built not just on our buildings, but on the experiences we deliver, the memories we help create, and the trust we continue to earn.

Mission

To provide guests with memorable experiences through exceptional service, world-class comfort, and inspiring design. We believe in personal touches, warm welcomes, and the magic of a stay that feels like home — wherever you are in the world.

We strive to be the preferred choice for travelers by offering consistently high-quality accommodations and tailored guest experiences. Our dedicated staff and state-of-the-art facilities ensure every stay is remarkable.

We aim to redefine the meaning of hospitality by continuously raising standards, embracing innovation, and focusing on details that matter to our guests. Every guest is more than a visitor — they are a valued member of the MNP family.

Our Values

  • Customer-first attitude
  • Integrity and transparency
  • Innovation and excellence
  • Global standards, local flavors
  • Sustainability and community care
  • Continuous learning and improvement
  • Respect for people and cultures
